SETUP:
Open the program and follow the prompts. Don't worry, it's not complicated.

TROUBLESHOOTING:
- Program doesn't launch at all
Disable your antivirus.
- PSO2 drops frames / runs slower
This is a Windows problem related to transparency on low-spec machines: move OverParse off your game, or disable the Always On Top option in the Window menu, and it should fix itself.
